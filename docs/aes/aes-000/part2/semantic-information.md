# AES-000 Part II — Semantic Information (`public/aquin-semantic.js`)

Shannon measures surprise; this measures MEANING + TRUTH. Node-tested (5).
- **Bar-Hillel & Carnap (1952)**: content = fraction of possible worlds EXCLUDED
  (tautology=0, precise claim high; contradiction=1 → the paradox).
- **Floridi (2004)** truth gate: strongly semantic information must be TRUE; a false
  claim is **misinformation** (it excludes the actual world), not information —
  asserting it is rejected.
- Educationally: a correct explanation narrows understanding toward truth (+info);
  a **misconception excludes the correct world** and is flagged harmful — why
  misconceptions are worse than mere ignorance.
