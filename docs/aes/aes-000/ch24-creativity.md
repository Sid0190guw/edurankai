# AES-000 Ch 24 — Educational Creativity (`public/aquin-creativity.js`)

Creativity = **novel AND valuable** (Boden; Runco & Jaeger), not randomness.
Node-tested. Grounded, no invented CS.
- **Combinational** (Boden): bridge DISTANT concepts; novelty = graph distance,
  value = structural overlap; noise (novel but valueless) filtered.
- **Analogical** (Gentner structure-mapping): solar-system↔atom falls out of
  matching relation types (systematicity), not surface features.
- **Scoring**: sun-planet = "too obvious", sun-electron = "novel but no valid
  bridge", sun-nucleus = "novel AND valuable" (creative).
Interface: `createCreativity().concept/relate/combine/analogy/score/distance`.
